Most of us do regular sport activities in our life, because we have lots of benefits of it: First of all sports are essential to be healthy, they are needed for enjoyment, they have a huge economical impact, and so on. After all doing sport is fun, but doing it alone sometimes makes it boring and monotonous, moreover there are lots of sport activities that cannot be done alone, they require one or more sport mates.

Doing any kind of sport activity is much more fun with somebody. We can learn from each other, we can motivate each other, and we can work harder in those certain competitive moments. Finding a good sport mate is not an easy task: We live in different locations with different time-tables. We have different fitness levels and different workout preferences.

Sport mate match offers a convenient way for finding the perfect sport mate for you. The only thing you need to do is to create your personal profile by fulfilling some simple form about your workout preferences and your fitness level in the preferred sport activities. After the profile is created you can browse across the community by selecting various filter conditions, and personal preferences.

So don’t forget: Sport is fun, but doing it in a great company is much more fun!

FAQ

What do I need for registration?
You only need a valid E-mail address.
For what kind of activities can I find a sport mate?
It contains all the popular activities and it can be extended with any suggested ones.
What is the guarantee that my mate will not skip the workout?
We cannot guarantee this as something unexpected can happen anytime, but everybody can be rated by the users therefore a notorious “skippers” can be filtered out.
Can I fill the fitness level of a given activity form in case I’m a newbie in that activity?
Yes because the content of the form is depends on your level of the certain activity. We ask more details from a professional than from a beginner.
Do I need to give personal details as my phone number and E-mail address to my public profile?
No because we have an in-build communication mechanism.
